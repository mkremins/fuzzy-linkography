Fuzzy Linkography

Below are some test examples of fuzzy linkographs: automatically generated diagrams of design or ideation sessions, highlighting semantic links between design moves that took place during the session.

As in conventional linkography, moves are arranged left to right in a single linear timeline. Links are drawn below moves to connect them. In fuzzy linkography, links are inferred by a computational process rather than hand-coded, so we use line color to indicate the strength of each link rather than treating all links as strictly binary.
